  ## **Introduction**


  **Tyendinaga Township Public Library (the “Library”) is seeking a qualified individual to provide casual IT support services on an as-needed basis. The successful IT professional will assist with troubleshooting, maintenance, upgrades, and other technology-related needs.**


  ## **Scope of Work**


  **The successful candidate will provide IT support services that may include, but are not limited to:**


  * **General computer, printer, and network troubleshooting**

  * **Software installation, configuration, and updates**

  * **Routine maintenance of computer systems and peripherals**

  * **Website support and updates**

  * **Wi-Fi and internet connectivity support**

  * **Data backup and recovery assistance**

  * **Hardware setup, replacement, or upgrade**

  * **Cybersecurity and antivirus management**

  * **Technology consultation or recommendations as requested**


  **Work will be scheduled on an as-needed basis. No minimum number of hours is guaranteed.**\

  **Qualifications**


  **Applicants should demonstrate:**


  * **Relevant post-secondary education or equivalent work experience in Information Technology**

  * **Experience supporting small organizations or public institutions preferred**

  * **Strong troubleshooting and communication skills**

  * **Knowledge of Windows operating systems, Microsoft Office, and networking principles**

  * **Ability to work independently and maintain confidentiality**


  ## **Term**


  **The contract will be for a one-year term, with the possibility of renewal based on performance and mutual agreement.**


  ## **Submission Requirements**


  **Proposals should include:**


  1. **Cover Letter – Introducing the candidate and outlining relevant experience**

  2. **Qualifications and Experience – Summary of skills, certifications, and previous contracts**

  3. **Hourly Rate – Include call-out fees (if applicable) and after-hours rates**

  4. **Availability – Typical response time and working hours**



  **All proposals must be submitted electronically to:

  [tyendinagatwplibrary@gmail.com](mailto:tyendinagatwplibrary@gmail.com)  by October 31, 2025 at 11:59 p.m.

  Late submissions will not be considered.**


  ## **Terms and Conditions**


  * **The Library is not bound to accept the lowest or any tender.**

  * **All work performed must comply with applicable laws and Library policies.**

  * **Contractors are responsible for their own insurance, equipment, and transportation.**

  * **Any confidential information obtained during work must not be disclosed.**
